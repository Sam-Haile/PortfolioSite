Key Features

With this game, I tried to capture the essence of the mid-2000s punk aesthetic. Jet grind Radio became a big influence on the art direction for this game. Infused with a vibrant and cartoony art style, the game's visuals transport you into a colorful world filled with graffiti, urban landscapes, and rebellious character designs, creating a visually captivating atmosphere.

Development

What can I do?

Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible as you collect notes and spray cans scattered throughout the level. The scoring system takes into account your accuracy in hitting the beats and your ability to strategically collect the spray cans. Strive for perfection and aim to get a new highscore!
