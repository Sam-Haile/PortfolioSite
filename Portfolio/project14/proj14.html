Objective

Our AR game was designed to push the boundaries of virtual interaction, creating a space where digital and physical space can work together seamlessly. We strived to create an experience where players can interact with the game universe as naturally as they would with the physical world around them.

My Role: UX Researcher

One of my key responsibilities was conducting comprehensive user surveys. These surveys were crucial in capturing insights about how users perceive and interact with AR technology as well as the genre of cooking simulators.

Outcome

The outcome of our AR gaming project showcased my ability to effectively translate user data into actionable design insights. I was able to contribute significantly to a project that, though hypothetical, demonstrated a deep understanding of user needs in an emerging technology space.

Understanding the Objective

Why are we doing this?

The core objective of our AR gaming project, presented as a hypothetical offering to a potential buyer, was a testbed for demonstrating advanced UX capabilities in a real-world context. Through the use of user surveys, I was able to get a comprehensive understanding of our target audience's experience levels and preferences, heres what I learned:

Our user surveys revealed that 100% of users had at least some interaction with AR/VR technology in the past, but only 30% were comfortable navigating the envviornment.

The original design of our game included competitive elements. However, user survey results indicated a preference for cooperative play and a relaxed gaming environment, with competitive play being one of the least desired features. In response to this, I led a pivot in our design strategy, abandoning the idea of competitive play and focusing on enhancing cooperative elements.

A significant challenge we faced was the initial misconception among players that our game would be minigame-based, while our vision was to create a more realistic simulator. To address this without losing our creative direction, I proposed incorporating mini-game-like features to boost players' scores. This solution effectively aligned user expectations with our original intent, ensuring user engagement while maintaining the desired realism.
