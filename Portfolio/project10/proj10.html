Attention to detail was very important during the development of this mod. To maintain a cohesive experience, the mod utilizes intelligent masking techniques that carefully separate the movie inspired visuals from the game's user interface elements. This approach ensures that the original interface design remains intact, allowing players to seamlessly navigate menus, track objectives, and fully engage with the game without any disruption.
