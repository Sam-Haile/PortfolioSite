What can I do?

During the development of the minigame, my focus was on seamlessly merging the graphical capabilities of SFML with the powerful physics engine of Box2D. I belive this created a cohesive and enjoyable gameplay experience. The result is a minigame where players control Kirby within a visually appealing arcade-like environment.

Kirby Minigame

Play Game View Repository

  • This minigame shows my ability in developing engaging gameplay logic for a physics-based game. The objective was to seamlessly combine the graphical capabilities of the SFML (Simple and Fast Multimedia Library) with the robust physics engine provided by Box2D. By leveraging external APIs and pre-built DLLs, I successfully integrated these two libraries, resulting in an immersive arcade-like experience.
