While the game is still being polished and all the levels are not completed. The main functionality is present and the player is able to interact with the world in a cohesive manner. Apart from enviornmental assets, all other assets were created by me.

Development

What can I do?

The player comes equipped with a sword that can strike in all directions, making sure any threat can be taken care of. In addition, the player is able to counter enemy attacks. If timed correctly, the enemy will recieve damage and be launched back. Stuck in a tight spot? Try jumping off walls or climbing them to evade your pursuers.
