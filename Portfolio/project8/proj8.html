Key Features

For this project, I wanted to Challenge myself by creating a larger piece of work. Texturing is by far my favorite process in the 3D modeling workflow as you can see your creation come to life. I paid close attention to detail and applied weathering effects, scratches, and dirt to convey the ruggedness and history of the mech. Through various texturing techniques and material adjustments, I successfully achieved a realistic and battle-hardened appearance, further enhancing the authenticity and narrative of the design.
