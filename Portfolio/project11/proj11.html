Key Features

Mechanically, both the investigators and the murderer have distinct abilities at their disposal to win the game. Investigators can collect clues while the murderer can create roadblocks, knock investigators unconscious or even kill them.

Development

What can I do?

At its core, 2 Weeks to Die is a combination of deduction and hidden information. Players take on the role of investigators, attempting to find various clues and find the murderer. To add an element of strategy, the murderer has various abilities that allow him to mislead investigators. Overall, the game requires players to think critically and strategically while also providing opportunities for social interaction and deception.
