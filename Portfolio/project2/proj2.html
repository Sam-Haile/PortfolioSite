Key Features

The player and enemies move using vector-based movement. All collisions are calculated using a mix of AABB (Axis-Aligned Bounding Box) and Circle collision. All art assets were created by me.

Development

What can I do?

The primary objective of this game is to survive waves of enemies while you collect as many stars as possible. Pick up health packs to keep your ship healthy and get the highest score you can!
