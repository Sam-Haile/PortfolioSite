Key Features

Shoot down waves of enemies and earn points to upgrade your abilities. This game incorporates a shop mechanic that allows the player to experiment with different upgrades, providing a different experience each time you play.

Development

What can I do?

The player has a gun to take down enemies at a distance. A wave is finished once the specified number of enemies are defeated. Enter the shop to purchase upgrades with the points you earned in the previous waves and aim for a new highscore!
